(Possibly) Interactive Webpage for the Premier Test Run of the TTT Board Game!

First, choose a character sheet!

(Click and hold to zoom)

Character Role Card

At the start of each round, each player would draw a role card.
The roles are:

  • Innocent: Your job is to solve the puzzle!
  • Traitor: Your job is to either confirm all the innocents have died or destroy 5 puzzle pieces.
  • (Optional) Jester Role: Your job is to get your self killed, but you can't do damage.
    If you are killed, swap role cards with your killer.
  • (Optional) Brown Role:
  • (Optional) Detective Role: You are an innocent. When you succeed on an investigation check of a body, you can reveal the role card to everyone.

Next, choose a Side Quest card

Choose at random, click to turn over.
Only click one card, as you will need to reload the page to reset.



We're ready to go! Here's the map

As we unlock game tiles, click to expand a space (add a tile to the map)

Wands!

When travelling around the map you might find a wand.
There are two types of wands: Attack Wands and Utility Wands.
When you find a wand, click on a wand card. One will appear in your 'inventory' space above.
Click on that image to display the spell. (spell card may appear far to the right, just scroll over)











Solving the puzzle

As you travel around the map you will collect puzzle pieces. Once you have revealed the Puzzle Room, you can try to solve the puzzle.
You need five puzzle pieces to be placed in the Puzzle Room to attempt to solve the puzzle.
Hopefully I will design a cool little puzzle thing with a variety of options and solutions.
Or it will just be a win condition.
The traitors can do something similar. They can win by destroying five puzzle pieces.
As with the innocents, the puzzle pieces must first be placed in the puzzle room.

Who won?

After some number of rounds, the player with the highest individual score wins.
