2. Overview of The Game


It's not Dnd, but you do get a character sheet

Your character has been invited to an elegant Christmas Ball!
It was all going so well until something oh-so sinister happened!
This elegant extravaganza has taken a turn for the worse.
The doors have been locked and the only way out seems to be some elaborate puzzle!

You must collect enough puzzle pieces from around the mansion and solve the puzzle quickly!
And watch your back, these Christmassy folks can't all be trusted.
There are traitors about looking to take all the points for themselves!


3. Setting up The Game


First, get all the pieces together

  1. Take a look through the character sheets. Everyone gets one.
  2. Then find the Ballroom Map item. Place it in the centre of the table.
  3. Shuffle the rest of the map tiles and place them face down in a pile. We will build the map as we go each round.
